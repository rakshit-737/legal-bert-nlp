A: Yes! That's the point - it's free exposure for your portfolio.

Q: Can I keep my code private?
A: Make the GitHub repo private, but Streamlit Cloud won't work with free tier.

Q: Will it auto-update when I push changes?
A: Yes! Streamlit Cloud watches your main branch automatically.

A: No! Streamlit Cloud handles all the infrastructure.
